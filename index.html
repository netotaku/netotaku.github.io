Profile

Nick is a Technical Director and award winning interface developer with a creative vision and a broad portfolio of skills and experience. He has an academic approach to technical project management and is a passionate exponent of open source.

He has extensive experience of working with destination brands such as Enjoy England, and public sector bodies such as the NHS and universities.

Specialising in Javascript and social integration, he has won awards for his interface work, and in recent times he has worked on major digital campaigns for brands such as Kia, Land Rover, Orange and the National Trust.

Nick has a passion for digital and is active in the Bristol technical community speaking on various topics including first principles of web development, SMM, SEO and Conversion.

He lives in Bristol with his wife Food Stylist Alison Clarkson and his two sons Daniel and Alex.
